# Privacy Policy for Qiblah

**Last updated: July 3, 2026**

## Overview

Qiblah is a Qibla direction finder and Islamic prayer times app developed
by al-umari labs. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Information We Collect

Qiblah requests access to your device's GPS location. This is used solely to:
- Calculate the direction of the Kaaba (Qibla) from your current position
- Compute the five daily Islamic prayer times for your location

## How Your Information Is Used

Your location is processed entirely on your device. It is:
- Never transmitted to any external server
- Never stored beyond the current app session
- Never shared with any third party
- Never used for advertising or analytics

## Information We Do Not Collect

Qiblah does not collect:
- Your name, email address, or any account information
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Usage data or analytics
- Any information from children

The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no
third-party tracking of any kind.

## Permissions

Qiblah requests the following Android permission:

**ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION** — to determine your GPS coordinates for Qibla
direction calculation and prayer times. You may deny this permission and
enter your coordinates manually instead; the app remains fully functional.

## Data Security

Since no personal data leaves your device, there is no data transmission
to secure and no server-side storage to protect.

## Children

This app does not target children and does not knowingly collect any
information from children under the age of 13.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same
